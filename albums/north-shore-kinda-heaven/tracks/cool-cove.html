About This Track

A neighborhood song for the west-end cove near Indian Beach, where everybody knows everybody else's dock, flags, grills, and boat lifts. It leans into the social fabric of the lake: wine moms, borrowed ice, paddle-boat commutes, and a shoreline that feels more like an extended porch than a subdivision.

About This Track

"Konocti Sunset" is the album's memory-burn track: a Clear Lake ballad for boat-in concert nights, old ticket stubs, and the particular ache of a place that still glows long after its era has closed. It anchors the record's larger theme that places can vanish in one sense and remain permanent in another.

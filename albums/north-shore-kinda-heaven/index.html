Album Intro

Recorded on the docks and decks of Clear Lake's North Shore, this album captures hundreds of summers in nine tracks plus a quiet outro. Country and Americana stories about the cove, the green dock, the boat lift, Konocti Harbor in the distance, and the blue house that held the whole thing together. As far as we've been able to find, the first full concept album devoted to Clear Lake.

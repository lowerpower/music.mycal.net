We Did Start the Fire (Burning Man History Jam)

“We Did Start the Fire (Burning Man History Jam)” is a rhythmic ritual, a timeline-in-flames, and a campfire chant for the dust-born. Tracing Burning Man’s evolution from a rope on Baker Beach to towering temples and art cars in the void, the song weaves names, myths, and moments into a lyrical memory loop — part oral history, part glitch-folk gospel, and a wink to the firestarter in all of us.

About this track

“We Did Start the Fire (Burning Man History Jam)” is a rhythmic ritual, a timeline-in-flames, and a campfire chant for the dust-born. Tracing Burning Man’s evolution from a rope on Baker Beach to towering temples and art cars in the void, the song weaves names, myths, and moments into a lyrical memory loop. It’s not just history — it’s oral tradition, glitch-folk gospel, and a wink to the firestarter in all of us.

“Speak Burner to Me” is a fast-talking Playa glossary in song form — a call-and-response tour through MOOP, Darkwads, Theme Camps, Arctica, DPW, and all the weird acronyms and in-jokes that make Black Rock City feel like another language.

About this track

“Speak Burner to Me” is the Black Rock City phrasebook turned into a track — a playful, high-density glossary of Burner slang, acronyms, and inside jokes. It’s half language lesson, half knowing wink: if you can sing along, you’re probably already Home.

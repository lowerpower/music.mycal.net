About this track

“Ash and Echoes (The Temple Song)” is the quiet center of the album — a hymn for the Temple, where feathers, photos, names and dust all meet the fire. It follows the walk in, the burn, and the strange lightness that comes after we let go of what we could never quite say out loud.

About this track

“Ten Principles, One Heart” is the Playa pledge turned into a chorus. It walks through the dust with a backpack of dreams and turns the principles into something you can shout with your camp: radical inclusion, gifting, immediacy, communal effort, and a reminder that when the burn is over, the real temple is the way we carry each other home.

About this track

“Burner Girl” is a desert crush anthem — a neon-lit love song about finding your orbit around a wild, glowing soul on the Playa. It lives where lasers, basslines, and dust storms collide: art cars rolling by, heartbeat to heartbeat, two burners spinning through the night like they were meant to meet there all along.

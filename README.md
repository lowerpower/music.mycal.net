
# music.mycal.net

**Official music site for Mycal** - Country-Americana stories from Clear Lake, folk-electronic music from Black Rock City, and other independent releases.

**Live site:** [music.mycal.net](https://music.mycal.net)

## New Album: North Shore Kinda Heaven

[**North Shore Kinda Heaven**](https://music.mycal.net/albums/north-shore-kinda-heaven/) is Mycal's new country-Americana concept album, released July 3, 2026.

Recorded on the docks and decks of Clear Lake's North Shore, its ten songs capture the cove, the green dock, the boat lift, Konocti Harbor, and the blue house that held hundreds of summers together. Marycal is featured on tracks 2 and 7.

**[Stream North Shore Kinda Heaven everywhere](https://distrokid.com/hyperfollow/mycal/north-shore-kinda-heaven)**

## About

This static website publishes albums, streaming audio, lyrics, credits, and track pages for Mycal's releases:

- **[North Shore Kinda Heaven](https://music.mycal.net/albums/north-shore-kinda-heaven/)** (2026) - ten country-Americana stories from Clear Lake's North Shore
- **[Ashes and Echoes - Songs from the Playa](https://music.mycal.net/albums/ashes-and-echoes/)** (2025) - fourteen folk-electronic songs about Burning Man culture, fire art, and transformation

## Technical Details

- **Stack:** Static HTML/CSS served by Caddy (no build process, no JavaScript required)
- **Structured Data:** Comprehensive JSON-LD on every page for discoverability
- **Performance:** Optimized for speed with no tracking pixels
- **License:** Code is MIT; music content is copyright 2025-2026 Mike Johnson (Mycal)

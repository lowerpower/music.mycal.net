About the format

Cronosonics came out of an accidental experiment: write an essay, distill its core ideas into a rough song, publish both, then notice readers quoting not just the prose but the lyrics when they needed the idea again. People forget bullet points. They don't forget choruses.

Cronofuture is the subset of that work pointed at the substrate questions — energy, computation, meaning, and the contest over the foundations underneath them. If you only listen to a few tracks here, start there.
